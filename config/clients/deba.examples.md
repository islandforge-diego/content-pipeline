## Notes on the voice (what makes these work)
- Hook is a short, real-tension line — often a question or a myth to bust.
- Deal breakdowns use clean bullet math; never promise returns, just show the example.
- Encouragement beat before the CTA ("you don't need to be perfect").
- IG/TikTok/FB CTAs are ManyChat comment-keywords; LinkedIn invites a conversation.
- Emojis are warm and sparing (🏡 🔑 ❤️ 🎤 👇), never noisy.
- Some content is **bilingual (Spanish)** — "paso a paso," family & future framing,
  free exhibition CTA. If the source is Spanish, write the caption in Spanish.
