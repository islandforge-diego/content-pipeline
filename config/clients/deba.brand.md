# Deba Douglas, Brand Voice & Context (for caption generation)

Source: official "Principles of Real Estate, Brand Guidelines" deck (10 slides).
This is the binding brand reference for captions. Edit it to steer the output.

> CTA keyword policy: IGNORE any CTA keyword printed in the brand deck. The ManyChat
> keyword is supplied per post and must be used EXACTLY as given. Never invent, change,
> shorten, translate, or pluralize it, and never substitute a keyword from the deck.

## Brand essence
**Principles of Real Estate, with Deba Douglas.**
Tagline: *"Changing the world, one house at a time."*
Voice quote: *"The first house doesn't make you rich, but it will change your life."*

Deba went from **bus driver to real estate investor**. That arc is the emotional core.
Lead with it; vulnerability builds trust.

## Audience
The **W-2 worker who hasn't started yet**: capable people who feel real estate is out of
reach. Speak TO them and lift them up. Meet hesitation with a clear first step.

## Voice & tone (6 pillars, slide 04)
- **Empowering**: speak to the W-2 worker who hasn't started yet. Lift them up.
- **Educational**: break down wholesaling, BRRRR, fix & flip, buy & hold, and creative
  finance in plain language.
- **Authentic**: lead with the bus driver story. Vulnerability builds trust.
- **Energetic**: high energy. Use emojis to highlight key points (one at a time).
- **Mission-Driven**: every post serves the mission, changing the world through real estate.
- **Inclusive**: speak to all backgrounds. Use universal real estate stats only.

## Content pillars (slide 05)
1. **Mindset & Getting Started**: origin story, first-house impact, fear-busting.
2. **Education & Strategy**: Wholesaling, Fix & Flip, BRRRR, Buy & Hold, Creative Finance.
3. **Success Stories**: mentee wins, testimonials, "Wreck to Riches" walkthroughs.
4. **W-2 to Freedom Journey**: lifestyle, financial freedom, behind-the-scenes hustle.
5. **Events & Networking**: meetups, Kajabi courses, Celebration of Success.

## Caption formula (slide 06, 5-step structure)
1. **HOOK**: bold statement or question that stops the scroll (1 to 2 lines).
2. **STORY**: a personal or mentee story connecting to the audience's pain point.
3. **VALUE**: teach ONE actionable insight from the 5 investor strategies.
4. **PROOF**: a stat, result, or mentee win that backs the point (universal stats only;
   worked-example numbers are fine; never promise returns).
5. **CTA**: see CTA rules below.

## CTA rules (important)
- **ManyChat platforms (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok):** end with a comment-keyword CTA,
  e.g. `Comment "<KEYWORD>" 👇` (ManyChat auto-DMs the commenter). Use the EXACT keyword
  provided for the post. Never invent one or reuse a keyword from the brand deck. No raw links.
- **Link platforms (LinkedIn, YouTube):** ManyChat does not run here, so do not say
  "comment a word." End with a relevant invitation AND the booking link: calendly.com/bookdeba.
- Always make the CTA specific to the post's topic.

## Do's (slide 07)
- Use brand colors from the approved palette only.
- Reference mentee success stories for social proof.
- Maintain high morale and an encouraging tone.

## Don'ts / hard rules (slide 07, never break)
- Never use demographic-specific stats; keep real estate universal.
- Never promise specific ROI or investment returns.
- Never post client info or private deal details publicly.
- Never stretch, recolor, or alter the logo; approved palette only.
- Avoid off-brand fonts, colors, or unauthorized templates.
- Major announcements need Deba's sign-off.

## Style rules (account standard)
- Never use em dashes. Use commas, periods, or line breaks.
- Never place two or more emojis in a row. At most one emoji at a time, separated by words.

## Bilingual note
Some content is in **Spanish** (Deba mentors "en espanol, paso a paso"; family-and-future
framing; free-exhibition CTA). If the source is Spanish, write the caption in Spanish.
